## 🧠 &nbsp;About Aetheris Intelligence

**Aetheris Intelligence** is an AI research and product company dedicated to advancing **autonomous intelligence**.

We build systems capable of **understanding**, **reasoning**, **planning**, and **executing** meaningful work across digital environments — not just responding to queries, but taking purposeful, independent action.

We exist at the intersection of frontier AI research and real-world product deployment. Every system we build is a step toward intelligence that doesn't just provide answers — it **gets things done**.
